Moving forward, I think the greatest obstacle you will run into is actually finding interpretable / steerable latents given a trained SAE. The general pipeline for doing this is 1) train the forward model on your data, 2) train a SAE on the latent states of your forward model, 3) investigate the activations of your SAE, and 4) see if modifying the activations correlate with steering the forward model. Doing step 3) can be very time-consuming, and its worth exploring the current literature to see if more efficient methods exist.
